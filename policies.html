    Late Policy

    For all appointments, clients are given a 15 minute grace period. If you have not arrived to your appointment after the grace period, your appointment will be cancelled. This rule is put in place for courtesy towards my other clients who's appointments may need to be pushed back to accomodate your late arrival and also accomodate my time. If no-call no-show your appointment, your deposit will not be refunded and you will be banned from booking again.

    Extra Guests

    Since I am a homebased stylist, there is a no extra guest policy in place to protect the safety and comfortability of me and others. All other guests are free to wait in the lobby or vehicle. For children above the ages of 2, feel free to reach out in advance and an exception may be made. Children under the age of 2 are always welcome no matter the circumstance.

    No Smoking

    Also because I am a home-based stylist, there is a no-smoking policy in place. This is to respect other guests in the space and the rules in my home. If a smoke break is necessary, feel free to reach out and ask for a break during your appointment and I will do what I can to accomodate you.

    Cancellations

    If your appointment is cancelled due to you not following a policy, your deposit will not be refunded. If I cancel your appointment due to a personal matter or mistake on my end, your deposit is automatically refunded or can be credited towards another appointment. If an appointment is cancelled by the cliente for any reason, it is my discretion of if their deposot is refunded.

    Washed Hair

    Because I do not have a proffessional salon, all clients are responsible for washing their hair before arriving to their appointments. Although it is NOT necessary for it to be blow dried or detangled if applicable.

    Wig Customizations

    When booking a wig service, please make sure the wig is dropped off to the address listed below at least 3 days in advance to the appointment. This allows me plenty of time to customize the wig before the appointment. Customizations include: plucking, dying, cutting, or bleaching knots.
