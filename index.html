Meet the Stylist

Hello, my name is Gia Ar'i, I am a hairstylist from Akron, Ohio located in Cincinnati, Ohio. I specialize in locs, wigs, braids and natural hairstyles. I am self-taught and learned how to do hair all on my own, it is through my clientele that I get the opportunity to learn more and more. I look forward to being your future hairstylist!
